About us

US Automation, a pioneer in the sector, offers complete corrosion protection solutions on all types of substrates in a variety of industries, including automobiles, hardware, electronics, white goods, jewellery, power supplies, aeronautics, railways, and defense, to name a few.

Our products span from traditional manual/automatic systems to specific applications such as plating on cycle spokes, cycle rims, the interior of gun barrels, and continuous lines for sheets/strips/wires.
